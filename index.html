About me

I am a Ph.D. student of the Computer Graphics Lab. at the University of Tokyo in Japan.
My main research interests focus on developing the progressive methods for rendering and animation of CG. I explore some promising techniques (e.g. quantum computing, machine learning, and so on) to improve the fundamental rendering or simulating algorithms.
