About me

I am a Ph.D. student of the Computer Graphics Lab. at University of Waterloo in Canada.
My main research interests focus on developing the progressive methods for rendering an animation of CG. I explore some promising techniques (e.g. quantum computing, machine learning, and so on) to improve the fundamental algorithms of rendering and simulating.
