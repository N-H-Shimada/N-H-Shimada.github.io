About me

I am a Ph.D. student of Computer Graphics Lab. at the University of Waterloo in Canada. My main research interest focuses on developing progressive methods for CG. I explore some promising techniques (e.g. quantum computing, machine learning, and so on) to improve the fundamental algorithms of rendering and simulation.
